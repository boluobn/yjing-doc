Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I forgot my PIN / gesture password. What should I do?
For privacy reasons, passwords are stored locally only and cannot be recovered. If you forget it, please uninstall and reinstall the app (note: this will erase in-app data).

Q: WiFi web management won't connect.
Make sure your iPhone and computer are on the same WiFi network. Open "WiFi Management" in the app and visit the address shown on screen.

Q: Will importing from the system Photos library delete the originals?
No. Import only copies files into YJing's private space. Originals in the system Photos library remain untouched.

Q: How do I restore my Pro subscription?
Go to Settings → Restore Purchases. If the issue persists, please contact us by email.

Privacy & Data

All YJing data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, or share your photos, videos, or files.
